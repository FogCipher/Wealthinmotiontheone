We use the information we collect to:

3. Pinterest Integration

We use the Pinterest API to publish and manage original content on our Pinterest business account. This integration is used solely for our own content distribution purposes. Specifically, we use the Pinterest API to:

  • Create and publish pins featuring our original content

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from Pinterest users through this integration. We do not access any third-party Pinterest accounts. Our use of Pinterest data is limited to managing our own business account and is subject to Pinterest's Developer Terms of Service and API Terms of Use.
